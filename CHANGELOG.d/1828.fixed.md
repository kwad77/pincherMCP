The IMPORTS, READS, WRITES and USES_VAR resolve passes now replace their edge sets atomically ([#1828](https://github.com/kwad77/pincher/issues/1828)), closing the same concurrent-reader gap #1772 fixed for CALLS. Each pass previously committed its `DELETE` and its re-`INSERT` as separate transactions; a concurrent `trace kinds=READS,WRITES` or `query` could land between them and see those edges momentarily absent. All four now route through `ReplaceEdgesByKindSource` (one transaction per kind).
