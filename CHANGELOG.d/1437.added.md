pinchQL supports `COUNT(DISTINCT n.property)` (`MATCH (a)-[:CALLS]->(b) RETURN COUNT(DISTINCT a.name)`) — the canonical "how many unique callers" query. Pre-fix this errored with a misleading "unbalanced delimiter". DISTINCT inside non-COUNT aggregates (SUM/AVG/MIN/MAX), `COUNT(DISTINCT *)`, and `COUNT(DISTINCT n)` (bare variable) all surface specific errors pointing at the supported shape (#1437).
